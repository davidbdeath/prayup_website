Vision

Churches and organizations gathering together to walk, pray, knock on doors, and help meet the needs of our community.

Plan

  • Prayer walk starts at 4p at your choosen start location ending downtown.
  • Walkers will be in groups of 5.
  • Some will walk and pray, others will knock on doors and ask residents if they need prayer.

Collective Worship

The end of the walk destination is a collective worship gathering downtown. Downtown there will be baptisms, food trucks, and opportunities for ministries to connect with people face to face.
